@startuml
actor Gmail as "Gmail"
participant GmailTrigger as "Gmail Trigger"
participant TextClassifier as "Classificador de Texto"
participant AIAgent as "Agente de IA"
participant GoogleCalendar as "Google Calendar"
participant WhatsAppAPI as "WhatsApp API"
participant TelegramAPI as "Telegram API"

Gmail -> GmailTrigger: Novo email detectado
GmailTrigger -> TextClassifier: Analisa relevância do email
alt Email irrelevante
    TextClassifier -> GmailTrigger: Marca email como "Não relevante"
    return
else Email relevante
    TextClassifier -> AIAgent: Gera resumo do email
    AIAgent -> GoogleCalendar: Cria/atualiza evento no calendário
    AIAgent -> WhatsAppAPI: Envia resumo ao WhatsApp
    AIAgent -> TelegramAPI: Envia resumo ao Telegram
end
@enduml
